About me¶

I am currently a PhD student at the University of Lleida (UDL) in the Research Group on Precision Agriculture (GRAP) - Department of Agricultural and Forestry Engineering (EAGROF). My work focuses on the application of photonic sensors, computer vision and spatial sampling methods to estimate fruit load in apple trees.
